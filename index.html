Carly Pecora is an actress, singer, and model living in New York City.

Carly Pecora is a recent graduate of the American Musical and Dramatic Academy, where she studied musical theatre. A Poconos, Pennsylvania native, Carly's love of theatre began at the age of 5 in a community theatre production of the Little Mermaid. Since moving to New York, Carly has made a number of appearances in theatre, film, and modeling.
